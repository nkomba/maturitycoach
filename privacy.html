Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Maturity Coach ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Eugene Nkomba, respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website at maturity.coach and the Maturity Coach app, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the site or the app, you agree to this policy.

Who we are

Maturity Coach is an executive coaching practice led by Eugene Nkomba. If you have any questions about this policy or your information, you can reach us at eugene@maturity.coach.

Information we collect

Information you give us

When you contact us through the website form or by email, we receive the details you choose to share — typically your name, email address, your role, and the content of your message. When you book a session, you provide scheduling details through our booking provider (see "Third-party services" below).

Information stored only on your device (the app)

The Maturity Coach app is designed to keep your personal reflections private. Anything you create in the app — journal entries, mood check-ins, self-assessment answers, and results — is stored only in your own browser or device, using local storage. This information is not transmitted to us, not stored on our servers, and we cannot see it.

Because your app data lives on your device, clearing your browser's site data, or uninstalling the app, will permanently delete those entries. We have no copy and cannot recover them.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider automatically records standard technical information when you visit — such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and the pages you view. This is used to keep the site secure, reliable, and working properly.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and communicate with you.
  • To schedule and deliver coaching conversations and services you request.
  • To operate, secure, maintain, and improve the website and app.
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Cookies and local storage

The app uses your browser's local storage — not tracking cookies — to save your entries on your device so they're there when you return. Our hosting provider may set essential cookies needed for security and performance. We do not use advertising trackers or sell your browsing activity.

Third-party services

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run the site and practice:

  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security. See the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Cal.com — appointment scheduling when you book a conversation. See the Cal.com Privacy Policy.
  • LinkedIn — if you follow links to our LinkedIn profile, LinkedIn's own policies apply.

These providers process information on our behalf or under their own policies. We encourage you to review their privacy notices.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to operate our services (for example, with the scheduling provider when you book), to comply with the law, or to protect our rights and the safety of others.

Data retention

We keep messages and contact details for as long as needed to respond to you and to provide our services, and as required by law. App data is retained on your own device until you delete it.

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information you have shared with us. To make a request, email eugene@maturity.coach. For information stored in the app, you are always in direct control — you can edit or delete any entry, or clear all of it, from your own device at any time.

Children's privacy

The website and app are intended for adults and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information under our control. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International visitors

We are based in the United States, and our providers may process information in the United States or other countries. By using the site, you understand your information may be processed in these locations.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected here.

Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your information? Email eugene@maturity.coach.
