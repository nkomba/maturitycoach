Gary Luehr

Empowering organizations to embrace Agile, and coaching teams to achieve great things — turning complexity into clarity and resistance into momentum.

Book Session With Gary
"Great organizations are built by great teams — and great teams are built by leaders who listen, empower, and remove obstacles."

Gary Luehr is a seasoned agile and leadership coach with over a decade of experience coaching and leading software teams in the aerospace industry. His focus is helping organizations embrace Agile methodologies and coaching teams to achieve great things — turning complexity into clarity and resistance into momentum.

Throughout his career, Gary has worked with major aerospace and defense organizations, including Northrop Grumman, where he guided distributed engineering teams through large-scale Agile transformations. His approach blends deep technical understanding with a human-centered coaching philosophy rooted in active listening, powerful questioning, and creating awareness.

As a Professional Certified Coach (PCC) through the International Coaching Federation and a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach (CPCC), Gary brings rigor and empathy to every engagement. His SAFe Program Consultant certification (SPC6) and Master of Science degree further ground his coaching in both agile frameworks and academic discipline.

Gary partners with executives, managers, and individual contributors alike to cultivate the mindset and practices that drive sustainable, high-performance delivery.
