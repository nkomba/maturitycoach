Book Session With Patrice

Patrice Nkomba (PCC, CPCC, Six Sigma Master Black Belt) is a partner coach and technical advisor at Maturity Coach, where she works with leaders navigating technical and organizational transformation. Her practice sits where delivery pressure meets leadership capacity — the point at which most transformation efforts either take hold or quietly stall.

Across more than thirty years, Patrice has coached leaders and teams through agile adoption, DevSecOps maturity, and the operational realities of change at scale. She works with executives who are accountable for outcomes they cannot personally deliver, and with the teams who carry that work day to day.
