About Cindy

Cindy McCown believes that we constantly write our stories—regardless of what events came before, or possibly because of them. Each of us has the extraordinary opportunity, every day and in each moment, to repair relationships, learn from failure, celebrate success, and envision and craft the next chapter. This philosophy anchors her coaching practice and resonates deeply with leaders navigating transitions, reinvention, or periods of rapid change.

Her proudest professional accomplishment was redirecting her journey to become a certified coach. Taking on that challenge and stepping into the unknown has brought vivid color and rich texture to her life in fascinating ways—and it's the same transformative energy she helps unlock in her clients. As a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach (CPCC) through The Co-Active Training Institute, Cindy combines rigorous coaching methodology with a storyteller's intuition and communicator's precision.

Before coaching, Cindy built a substantial career as a copywriter, editor, and media producer, working with diverse clients across marketing, communications, promotions, and advertising. With a degree in broadcast journalism and extensive experience in video production, television, film, and digital media, she developed an exceptional ability to create engaging content that is brand-relevant and meets client business needs. Today, she applies those same skills to helping leaders find their voice, clarify their message, and communicate with impact.

In her own words, Cindy was "most likely to get in trouble at school for too much chit-chat"—she's inquisitive and loves to talk, listen, and ask questions. The only adjustment was learning her timing. That same curiosity now serves her coaching practice: asking powerful questions that surface insights, creating space for reflection, and partnering with clients to discover answers that are uniquely their own.
